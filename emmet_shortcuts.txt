My custom keybinds:
[
Wrap with abbreviation: ctrl + k & ctrl + shift + a,
Remove Tag: ctrl + r & ctrl + t
]

Wrap with abbreviation
https://docs.emmet.io/actions/wrap-with-abbreviation/

Remove Tag
https://docs.emmet.io/actions/remove-tag/

To set up the keybinds:
go to keyboard shortcut settings (ctrl + k & ctrl + s)
type: Emmet:<action>, in my case: emmet:Wrap with abbreviation
click plus sign and type your custom shortcut. done!
